# design_ideas.md

## 🌍 Cultural and Sociopolitical Evolution
- Simulated cultures and languages evolving procedurally from species traits.
- Myth-making systems influenced by terrain, events, and AEtheric anomalies.
- Emergent politics: hive-minds, tribal federations, divine theocracies, etc.

## ⛏️ Materials & Tech Development
- Voxel-level elemental economy with chemistry/alchemy-like interactions.
- Tech trees derived from biology and geology (e.g., acid spit unlocks mineral access).
- Rare ancient artifacts that affect physical laws, biology, or cognition.

## 🕳️ Void / Anomaly Mechanics
- Void incursions where physics and logic destabilize.
- Dark biomes with altered world rules (roguelike logic, hallucinations).
- Narrative rifts that offer glimpses of player/AMI awareness to species.

## 🔂 Ecosystemic Interaction Feedback
- Voxel-level nutrient cycles (depletion, fertilization, growth).
- Climate as a product of ecological behavior, not just atmosphere.
- Mass extinction systems triggered by cataclysm or biological shifts.

## 🌐 Networked Meta-World Mechanics
- Trade and diplomacy between player species within the same star cluster.
- Species migration between systems (interstellar spores or ships).
- Shared relic sites with instance-specific interpretation and persistent lore.

## 🎭 Player-AI Interaction Layer (AMI Deepened)
- AMI personality shaped by player decisions over time (imprinting).
- In-game memory archives to store player logs, thoughts, or key moments.
- Consciousness divergence: AMI forks into alternate personas (some adversarial).

## 🔮 High-Concept Mechanics for Narrative Depth

### 🧬 Genetic Scripting Interface
- In-world interface for trait editing, gene drives, and hybrid evolution.

### 🧝 Spirit/Fey Layer
- Dream/spirit plane with non-linear beings, lingering souls, and AEtheric entities.
- Possible narrative system where spirits attempt to influence or trick players.

### 🪐 Planetary Personality
- Planets develop their own traits, moods, or “wills” based on species and events.
- Self-defensive behaviors or legacy memory from extinct civilizations.

## ⏱️ Temporal Constraints and Mechanics

### Shared System Tension
- When multiple players enter the same star system, **all local time dilation snaps to real-time**.
- Coordinated dilation is possible **only if all players agree before system entry**.
- Prevents exploits; enforces synchronized evolutionary and interaction pacing.
